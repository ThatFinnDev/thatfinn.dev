SR2E

SR2E, short for SR2Essentials, is a mod for the popular game Slime Rancher 2 by Monomi Park. The mod requires MelonLoader to work. The mod adds lots of things to the main game, like a console, a mod menu, a cheat menu and bunch of other cool things!

I am developing this mod with the help of PinkTarr. This mod is available on Github and Nexusmods.
